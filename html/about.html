About Henry's Painting and Drywall

My name is Henry Estrella, I am the owner/operator of Henry's Painting and Drywall right here in San Jose, CA. I have been painting the inside and outside of homes around the San Jose area for the last 20+ years, improving my craft one job at a time.

I started this company to help homeowners with their home renovations so they could have a trustworthy, honest, and most of all affordable house painter who can come and knock out those projects without knocking out their bank account.

I am also a drywall specialist offering services from texturing drywall, repairing drywall, and installing all new drywall in a newly renovated room or new home construction.
